FAQ

Common Questions

Do you provide a free quote?
Yes — all quotes are completely free and obligation-free. We'll assess your space and provide a detailed, transparent quote with no hidden fees.
Are your staff insured and background-checked?
Absolutely. All Faith Property Services staff are fully insured, background-checked, and have completed our comprehensive training program before working on any site.
Do you use eco-friendly cleaning products?
Yes. Wherever possible, we use environmentally responsible, biodegradable cleaning products that are safe for your staff, clients, and the planet.
What areas do you service?
We service all of Victoria, Australia and also operate in New Zealand. Contact us to confirm coverage in your specific area.
Can you accommodate after-hours cleaning?
Yes — we're flexible and can work around your schedule, including early morning, evening, and weekend cleans to minimise disruption to your business.
How quickly can you start?
In many cases we can begin within 48–72 hours of accepting a quote. For larger contracts, we'll agree on a start date that works for your team.
